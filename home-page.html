CATEGORY What Is League of Legends Coaching? 20/07/2020 We’ll keep this section short, as you might already know what coaching is. If you do, then feel free to skip it.
Okay, now, for those of you who are sthere, League of Legends coaching is defined as: “Receiving assistance from a player of a higher skill level with the purpose of improving your own ability.” This assistance can come in a couple of forms, but the standard coaching involves going over replays together. You join a
voice call with your coach, and then you sit down and watch a game you’ve played recently. This replay is usually a
defeat, even if watching a defeat doesn’t make you feel as cool, as there’s more knowledge to be gained from a defeat.
